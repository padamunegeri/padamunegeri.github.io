24. ...fandi...his employee are not Coding to the meeting


Jawaban:

Correlative conjunction yang tepat untuk kalimat tersebut adalah

- Both Fandi and his employee are not coming to the meeting.

- Not only Fandi but also his employee are not coming to the meeting.

Penjelasan:

Kalimat di atas terdiri dari

1. Fandi is not coming to the meeting.

2. Fandi's employee is not coming to the meting.

Jika digabung:

- Both Fandi and his employee are not coming to the meeting. (baik Fandi dan karyawannya tidak datang ke pertemuan)

- Not only Fandi but also his employee are not coming to the meeting. (tidak hanya Fandi, tetapi juga karyawannya tidak datang ke pertemuan)

32. ...fandi...his employee are not Coming to the meeting


Jawaban:

Correlative conjunction dari soal ... fandi ... his employee are not coming to the meeting yaitu

Not only Fandi but also his employee are not coming to the meeting.

Bila diartikan: tidak hanya Fandi tetapi juga pegawainya tidak datang ke pertemuan.

Penjelasan:

Ada 4 correlative conjunction yang biasa digunakan, yaitu

1. both ... and ...

2. not only ...but also ..

3, either ... or ...

4. neither ... nor ...

Jawaban lainnya yaitu

Both Fandi and his employee are not coming to  the meeting.

Bila diartikan: Baik Fandi dan karyawannya tidak datang ke pertemuan.

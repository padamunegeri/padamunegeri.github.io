28. Summarize the four major development!


The four major development are four domains that develop since the moment of a child's birth. These developments are interdependent. They influence one another.
These four developments are physical, cognitive, language and social & emotional developments.
 
The physical development is the development of motor skills. There are two kinds of motor skills, fine motor skills and gross motor skills.
Fine motor skills are small muscle movements that occur in the finger and in coordination with the eyes. These skills develop with time and practice.
Gross motor skills are the movement of the larger muscles like walking, running, throwing, etc. These skills relate to body awareness, reaction speed, balance and strength.

The cognitive development is the development in learning and processing information. It involves the development of language, mental imagery, memory, thinking, reasoning and problem solving. This development has to be learned because it is not an automatic process.

Language development is the development of communication ability. This allows us to express ourselves. Language  development includes verbal, like phonology and semantics,  and non verbal skills such as gestures and body language.

Social and emotional development gives children the ability to empathise with others and control their own feelings. This teaches a child to play well with others, share, take turns and accept differences. These are mostly learnt by observing.

1. Syntax means a set of grammar rules on words or clauses in forming sentences. Usually, in written sentences and conversations.
2. Deep structure is a level of syntactic representation with a number of properties that need not necessarily go together.: Major grammatical relations, such as subject of and object of, are defined at deep structure.

3.Structure ambiguity is the presence of two or more possible meanings within a single sentence or sequence of words.

4. Recursion occurs when a thing is defined in terms of itself or of its type. Recursion is used in a variety of disciplines ranging from linguistics to logic.

5 tree diagram is a diagram used in strategic decision making, valuation or probability calculations.

6. Symbols used in syntactic description Symbols are abbreviations for parts of speech and other constituents of sentences.

7. lexical rule is in a form of syntactic rule used within many theories of natural language syntax. ( argument structure)

8 Movement rule is.the means by which some theories of syntax address discontinuities.  or

9. Back to recursion is beautifully defined here: “How to design an algorithm to solve a problem by solving a smaller instance of the same problem.

10. Complement phrase is word, phrase or clause that is necessary to complete the meaning of a given expression. Complements are often also arguments (expressions that help complete the meaning of a predicate).

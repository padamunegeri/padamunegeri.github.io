6. tilong di hawab please........


Answer: 

In spotting errors and correcting mistakes, you need to have a great understanding of English grammar. Here are the correction:
a. Professor Jameson is extremely well-liked by his colleagues. (should be using 'extremely' as adverb)
b. The report was concise and succinct. (should be combined directly as both are equally the same)
c. The picture had fallen behind the sofa. (should be using V3 after 'had' to make past perfect)
d. They spend their vacation either in Europe or Mexico. (should be 'vacation' without 'suffix -s')
e. Beside having two houses in the city, he owns a farm in the country. (should be adding 'Ving' before 'two houses')
f. Is that ruby-colored grape grown in Rio Grande Valley? (should be in this order and in passive voice)
g. After he finished dinner, the telephone rang. (should be added subject in the sub clause) 
h. She had no idea who had left her the note. (should be 'who' as it refers to subject pronoun, not object)
i. They agreed to make a decision tomorrow. (should be in this order as time signal / adverb of time should be placed at the end of the sentence)
j. The sale of antique furniture is going well even though it's expensive. (should be using present continous as it tells about the trend)

Jawab:

Besar ∠C = 60°

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

∠B = 4/5 ∠C

∠A = 3/2 ∠B

∠A = 3/2 × (4/5 ∠C)

∠A = 6/5 ∠C

∠A + ∠B + ∠C = 180°

6/5 ∠C + 4/5 ∠C + 5/5 ∠C = 180°

3 ∠C = 180°

∠C = 180° ÷ 3

∠C = 60°

[ JAWAB ] B. 60°

[ PENJELASAN ]

DIKETAHUI :

perbandingan A dan B = 3 : 2

perbandingan B dan C = 4 : 5

LANGKAH :

1. mencari perbandingan ketiganya

2. mencari sudut C

PENGERJAAN :

1. A : B : C = 3 . 4 : 4 . 2 : 2 . 5

A : B : C = 12 : 8 : 10 → 6 : 4 : 5

2. 180 = sudut A + B + C

[tex] \frac{c}{a + b + c} \times 180 \\ \frac{5}{6 + 4 + 5} \times 180 \\ \frac{5}{15} \times 180 \\ = 60[/tex]

17. Tolong hawab lah soal ini 3^x-3^(-x+1)+4=0

3ˣ - 3.3⁻ˣ + 4 = 0

misalkan 3ˣ = y

y - 3/y - 4= 0 ...kalikan dengan y

y² - 3 - 4y = 0 atau y² - 4y - 3= 0

(y - 3)(y - 1)= 0

y = 3 atau y = 1

3ˣ = y --> 3ˣ= 3 atau 3ˣ = 1

3ˣ = 3¹ atau 3ˣ = 3⁰

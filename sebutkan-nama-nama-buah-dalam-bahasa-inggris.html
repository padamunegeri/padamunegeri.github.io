Understanding animals is very tempting to be discussed, every animal in this world has a specific environment, the environment determines their (animals) characteristic, food, activities, and many more. In this time, I am going to mention them (animals) for you, and their habitat, food, activities, and their characteristic.

Pembahasan

1. Lion; they are crowned as a king of the jungle, lions live in an open desert, with their colonies, they have their food from a prey, after killing their prey they take its flesh to be eaten. Lion has a huge mane which makes their look stronger, but lioness doesn't.

2. Wolf; wolf lives by building colony like lion, but their habitat is not in the desert, they prefer forest than desert that's why their bodies are full of nice fur, wolves eat flesh like lion, they are carnivore

3. ant; unlike the preceding animals, ant's colonisation is built by one queen, the elder one from them, ant can eat almost everything but they are famous animal which eat sweet foods, their bodies is small and is divided into 3 books, the front book is their head, the middle one is chest and back, and the back one is stomach

4. Penguin; I like them very much because they are the most loyal animal for their partner, penguins can't fly but they are the only bird with amazing swimming skill, penguins eat fishes from the bottom of frozen lake and sometimes they hunt fishes in the sea. their bodies are so cute, but don't underestimate them because they can attack you if you disturb

5. Blue Whale; I think it's the biggest animal in this era, whales are an independent animal, they don't have colonies or gang or something like that, they just swim alone in an open ocean, whales eat planktons and other small organisms

Next, I'm going to simplify my explanation

6. Horse; its habitat is meadow; it eats grass, straw, hay; it's four legged animal which can run fast

7. Rabbit; its habitat is meadow and forest; it eats grass, straw, hay; it's four legged; they have smooth fur on their small bodies

8. Squirrel; its habitat is forest; they eat nuts; they are good jumper, they can jump from tree to tree easily; their bodies is like rabbit but longer, with rusty brown colour, and long tail

9. Octopus; they live in ocean; they eat small fishes; they have good camouflage skill; they have no bones so they can bend their bodies.

10. Turtle; its habitat is open sea; they are carnivore, they eat flesh; they swim to hunt; turtle has strong body like rock, four legged, and unique

11. Lizard; they live in a forest, usually we can find on tree benches; they are insectivore, they eat insects or bugs like mosquitoes and flies, also moths; they have good camouflage skill like octopus; they have four leg and sticky tongue

12. Snake; they live in jungle, or some time we can find them in forest; they eat meat, usually mice's flesh; they are creeper as they have no limb; snake is the most simplest animal

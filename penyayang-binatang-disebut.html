40. jelasakan binatang kesayangan kelinci


Rabbits are classified as rodent mammals such as hamsters, guinea pigs, and mice. This animal that is known to be very cute and tame has a long ear size, mustache, and has very strong legs to jump. But rabbits are easier to identify with the size of their long ears. Ear size in rabbits can reach 10 cm. This animal ear also has a function to detect the appearance of predators that will eat it. These long-eared animals are able to hear the sound of predators with predictions of the right range and accuracy. His ears also function as guards for his body's heat, especially when winter arrives. Rabbits have very strong legs. With both legs, the rabbit is able to jump with a range of up to one meter. The rabbit leap is also a sign of the danger signal conveyed to the group in the wild.


Because these rodents are very cute and tame, no wonder rabbits are one of your favorite pets. When used as a pet, of course we provide cages and food for him. Without understanding what rabbit life is like in its natural habitat. In the wild, rabbits live in tunnels as their place of residence. The place is very good for growing and protecting yourself from predatory animals. Rabbits don't just dig one hole, they dig some tunnels to make it easier to hide if predators come.


Rabbits are very popular among pet lovers around the world, including in Indonesia. In the past few years the number of rabbit keepers has increased rapidly and up to now one million rabbits of various types have been maintained by many people. Like cats, hamsters, guinea pigs and other pets, rabbits are also considered as pets that can reduce the stress burden on their owners.


In keeping rabbits you should not keep them locked in a cage. This is because these animals are easily bored if they are in the same place for a long time. Place this animal in an open yard that allows this cute animal to play. You also need to find another rabbit as a playmate for your pet rabbit. Because rabbits are basically animals that live in groups and communicate with each other like animals. You can imagine if he was alone, then this funny animal would be easily stressed and sick.


Kelinci merupakan hewan peliharaan yang menggemaskan dan imut. Alasan inilah yang membuat banyak orang tertarik memelihara kelinci.

Anak-anak pun biasanya senang melihat dan bermain dengan kelinci. Kelinci yang melompat-lompat membuat anak terus tergerak untuk mengejarnya.

Selain itu, dibandingkan hewan peliharaan lainnya, kelinci cenderung lebih “pendiam” dan tidak menimbulkan suara yang membuat satu rumah bising.
